what the...

ba.dg.es will (eventually) show you all your badges across all your social networks.

Why??

I wanted to play with some of the social networking endpoints. I like badges, too. This is something of an exercise in how NOT to write a modern web app - I didn't use any frameworks and it really shows.

How??

First, log in with Facebook - that way I can keep track of who you are across multiple networks.

Then, click any network's button to connect or refresh your badges from that site. For example, click on FourSquare to connect to your FourSquare account.
